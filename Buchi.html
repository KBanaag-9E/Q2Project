Buchi or Butsi

Good for 3

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups sweet rice flour
  • ¾ cup water
  • ¼ cup sweet red bean paste
  • ½ cup sesame seeds
  • 2-3 cups cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the sweet rice flour and water then mix well

    2. Note: This should form a dough.

  2. Separate the dough into individual pieces
  3. Roll each individual piece of dough until a spherical (round) shape is formed
  4. Press the center of the spherical dough until the shape becomes concave

    5. Note: There should be a hollow spot in the center.

  5. Seal the dough and roll it once more until the shape is spherical
  6. Place the sesame seeds in a bowl then roll the sphere shaped dough over it. Make sure that the seeds stick on the dough
  7. Heat the pan and pour-in the cooking oil
  8. When the cooking oil is very hot, deep fry the dough until the sesame seed's color turns golden brown (about 2 to 3 minutes)
  9. Remove the cooked buchi from the pan and drain excess oil
