About Pinoy Chomp

Welcome to Pinoy Chomp! This webpage aims to compile the vast amount of Filipino recipes from around the internet and submissions on our form. These works are categorized into several sets, which we hope to add more to in the future.

All of the images seen are drawn by the creator of this page (Kyla Banaag). Most of these artworks are inspired by Vanjo Merano (creator of Panlasang Pinoy) and the other individual recipe sites referenced.
