Fruit Salad

Good for 8-10

Ingredients

  • 1 can fruit cocktail in light syrup (796 ml)
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp sweetened condensed milk (add more if you want it sweeter)
  • 1 bottle nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 1 bottle coconut strings
  • Optional: Â¼ cup cheese (cut into small squares)

Instructions

  1. Using a strainer, drain the fruit cocktail. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine heavy whipping cream and condensed milk.
  3. Using a hand mixer, whisk until soft peaks form.
  4. Add the fruit cocktail. Mix.
  5. Add nata de coco (coconut gel) and coconut strings. Mix.
  6. Optional: Mix in the cheese.
  7. Refrigerate or chill at least four hours. Serve cold.
