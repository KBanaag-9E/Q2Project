Buko Pandan

Good for 4

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
20 oz young coconut strips
250 ml Nestlé all purpose cream
150 ml Nestlé carnation condensada
2 cups water
3 oz powdered gelatin
1/2 Ib pandan leaves
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp buko pandan flavoring
1/2 cup cooked sago pearls (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a saucepan, then add pandan leaves and cover, continuing to boil for 15 minutes. Then, remove the leaves. (½ lb. pandan leaves, 2 cups water)
  2. Next, add sugar, powdered gelatin, and buko pandan flavoring, stirring until the powder is completely dissolved in water.
  3. Turn off the heat and transfer the mixture to a mold, allowing it to cool until firm. For quicker results, chill in the refrigerator. (3 ounces powdered gelatin, ¼ cup sugar, ½ teaspoon buko pandan flavoring)
  4. The texture of the mixture should be firm once cooled. You may also place this inside the refrigerator for faster results

    5. Note: Allow the temperature to go down before putting it in the refrigerator.

  5. Combine young coconut strips, Nestlé Carnation Condensada, Nestlé All Purpose Cream, and cooked sago pearls in a bowl. Chill in the refrigerator or freezer for a few hours until the texture thickens. (250 ml Nestlé All Purpose Cream, 150 ml Nestlé Carnation Condensada, ½ cup cooked sago pearls, 20 ounces young coconut strips)
  6. Allow the texture to thicken by chilling in the refrigerator or freezer for a few hours.
  7. Slice the firm gelatin into ½ inch cubes. Combine the gelatin cubes with the creamy mixture of cream, sago pearls, and condensed milk.
  8. Transfer to individual serving platters or cups, and optionally top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
