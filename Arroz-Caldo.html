Arroz Caldo

Good for 6

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lbs chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 34 ounces water (about 1 liter)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup onion (minced)
  • 4 pieces hard boiled eggs
  • 1 cup scallions (green onions, minced)
  • 2 knobs ginger (julienned)
  • 3 tablespoon safflower (kasubha)
  • 1 piece lemon or 4 pieces calamansi
  • 1 chicken cube (bouillon)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat the cooking oil then sauté the garlic, onion, and ginger
  2. Dash-in some ground black pepper
  3. Add the ox tripe and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add the chicken cube and cook until the cube melts
  5. Put-in the chicken and cook until outer layer color turns golden brown
  6. Add the fish sauce and uncooked rice then mix and cook for a few minutes
  7. Pour-in the water and bring to a boil
  8. Stir occasionally and simmer until the rice is fully cooked (about 30 to 40 minutes)
  9. Put-in the hard boiled eggs
  10. Add the safflower for additional color and aroma
  11. Serve hot with garlic, minced scallions, and lemon.
