Arroz Caldo

Good for 6

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 1/2 lbs chicken (cut into serving pieces)
1 cup uncooked rice
34 oz (about 1 liter) water
2 tbsp fish sauce
1 tsp garlic
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 cup onion (minced)
4 pcs hard boiled egg
1 cup scallion (green onions, miced)
2 knobs ginger (julienned)
3 tbsp safflower (kasubha)
4 pcs calamansi
1 pc chicken cube (bouillon)
2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  6. Add the fish sauce and uncooked rice then mix and cook for a few minutes
  7. Pour-in the water and bring to a boil
  8. Stir occasionally and simmer until the rice is fully cooked (about 30 to 40 minutes)
  9. Put-in the hard boiled eggs
  10. Add the safflower for additional color and aroma
  11. Serve hot with garlic, minced scallions, and lemon.
